About

I am a Business Management graduate specialising in Innovation and Information Technology, with a strong interest in business analytics, data-driven decision-making, and digital transformation. My academic and project experience sits at the intersection of business strategy and technology, where I focus on using data, systems, and analytical tools to solve real organisational problems. Throughout my studies, I have developed practical skills in Python, SQL, Power BI, Excel, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, alongside a solid understanding of business processes, innovation frameworks, and digital strategy. I particularly enjoy working with data - from cleaning and modelling datasets to building dashboards and insights that support evidence-based decision-making.

This website showcases selected projects that reflect my ability to translate business requirements into technical solutions. Each project demonstrates structured problem-solving, analytical thinking, and a focus on practical outcomes rather than theory alone. Where possible, I aim to design solutions that are scalable, user-focused, and aligned with organisational goals.

I am currently progressing towards a career in business intelligence and data analytics, with long-term ambitions in data engineering and digital innovation. I am committed to continuous learning, professional development, and applying technology in ways that create measurable value for organisations.
